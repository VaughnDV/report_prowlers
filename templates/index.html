What does reportprowlers do exactly?


A community driven communication system, whereby users can simply report suspected prowlers, vehicles, groups and photos to their local security services, who can then in turn, be at the right place at the time to prevent, and deter crimes in our areas.

Report Prowlers is an internet based service designed to advance security in neighborhoods through simplified online communication between the neighborhood's residents, and the local security services.


Who can use this service?


Any one who signs up can send reports free of charge. Subscribers (such as Security companies) support this system financially through monthly subscription, and recieve the prowler reports.


What are prowlers?


A prowler is a person who is in your area with intent of seeking our criminal opportunities.


How it works:


USERS = Area's Residents and Security Personal on patrol

Users login, and send reports of suspicious activity in their area. Its free, and easy to use. From the USERS perspective, the process is as follows:

Login > Send Report > Safer Neighborhood



SUBSCRIBERS = Area's Security Services:

Receive these reports, with additional intelligence on comparisons with other recent reports, recommended response level. Subscribers pay a monthly subscription fee for each area they wish to monitor:

Monitor Emails > Receive Reports > Allocate Appropriate Response

What do we do with the information you provide?


The information is recorded, compared to similar reports, formatted and sent to subscribing security companies for that area. The reports are stored in our databases for 72hours before they are archived


Soon we will introduce facial recognition in comparing the image reports, with the intent of identifying known offenders early, and a mobile app to provide a more convenient user experience


What makes this system special is that it is uses realtime data, driven in unity with the people, homes and families we aim to best protect. Security personal can use the same system, thus all the information is readily availible, allowing patterns to be identified faster, ensuring the correct level of response at the right time.


The ReportProwlers system is designed to run concurrently with existing security operations or initiatives. This enables us to provide this service to other security companies that wish to support and join our quest for safety without compromise.


We believe this is the future of the security industry however and its still under active development. Please do not hesitate to contact us with your suggestions on how we can improve out service to you.
